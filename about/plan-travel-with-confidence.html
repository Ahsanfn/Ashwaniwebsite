Plan with Confidence

Travel built with

Our travelers are at the heart of everything we do. That’s why our dedicated staff works tirelessly to make the dream of travel a reality. With easy payments, stress-free booking options, 24/7 global support (and so much more), our expertly crafted tours are always within reach. We’ve also introduced new flexible policies so you can plan your next trip with extra assurance and travel when the time is right for you.

Only $99 down & no payments for 60 days

Making travel possible for everyone is what inspires us. Enroll in AutoPay when you book, and you can hold a spot on your dream trip with just a $99 deposit, and wait 60 days before your first interest-free payment.†

Learn more

Fully Refundable Booking Period through February 28

Book your tour now through February 28, 2021 and feel good about your decision. If you change your mind, you’ll receive a refund of all money paid (including your deposit!) through May 1.†

Flexible, fee-free rebooking options

Need to make new travel plans? We’ve got you covered. You can easily change your tour date or destination up until your Final Payment Date.† It’s just one more way we put you at the center of everything we do.

The EF COVID Care Promise

Additional support provided to all travelers

If a traveler is either diagnosed with COVID-19 while on tour or required to quarantine on account of COVID-19 while on tour, EF will provide or arrange for support including lodging, meals, translation services, and more.
