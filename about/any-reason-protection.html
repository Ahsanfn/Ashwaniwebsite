Cancellation protection
Feel good about planning that dream tour. If needed, you can cancel anytime prior to checking in for your first flight and receive a voucher for future travel.

No fees for rebooking flights
AnyReason Protection also includes flights you’ve reserved with Go Ahead Tours, which means you won’t be stuck with airline fees if you need to reschedule your trip.

For anything—really
That’s right with AnyReason Protection there are no exclusions. It’s simply great travel coverage you can count on for every great Go Ahead tour you plan.

Easy to add. Easy to use.

AnyReason Protection lets you make your travel plans with confidence. Add it to either of our Travel Protection Plans for just $75 and you can cancel your trip anytime prior to checking in to your first flight.

But don’t wait—the deadline to get AnyReason Protection for most tours is 70 days before departure (90 days for select tours). Select it today during online checkout or give us a call at 1-800-597-0350.
