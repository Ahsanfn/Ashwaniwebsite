Corporate partnerships

Request more info

We partner with organizations looking to make a difference in their members’ lives, whether it's on alumni travel tours, trips with local banks, special interest tours through community organizations, or spiritual tours through religious organizations. By working with company and community leaders to design personalized experiences for travelers, we help our partners build relevancy, spread awareness, and create unforgettable experiences for their group members.

An example of how we work together
EF Go Ahead Tours + Ancestry®

We've collaborated with Ancestry®, the go-to resource for people looking to explore their family story, to create a brand-new portfolio of tours. These Heritage Tours offer travelers all the perks of a traditional Go Ahead Tour, plus valuable, genealogical-focused benefits like an on-tour expert from AncestryProGenealogists® and opportunities to learn about emigration.

Designing a custom itinerary
A look at Edible Boston's tour

The culinary magazine Edible Boston worked with us to put together a customized Food & Wine Tour through the hidden gems of Northern Italy, which they marketed to their readers. Knowing that their audience is most excited about off-the-beaten-path experiences, we included special foodie-worthy activities, like foraging for truffles and visiting a balsamic vinegar estate.
